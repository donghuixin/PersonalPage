Overview

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the research group led by Prof. Qian Zhang at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Previously, I obtained the PhD degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) under the supervision of Prof. Wei Wang in MetaIoT Lab.

My research focuses on backscatter networking, wireless sensing, and low-power IoT systems, with publications in top-tier conferences such as MobiCom, SenSys, and Ubicomp (IMWUT).
