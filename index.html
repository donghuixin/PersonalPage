Research Overview

My research focuses on the intersection of backscatter networking and wireless sensing, with a particular emphasis on developing low-power, commodity-compatible technologies for the Internet of Things (IoT). Key contributions include extending GPS positioning to indoor/shadowed regions via backscatter, enabling passive sensing with pervasive GNSS signals, and optimizing BLE backscatter for seamless integration with commercial devices. My work aims to address core challenges in IoT scalability, energy efficiency, and environmental adaptability, with applications in smart agriculture, emergency rescue, and industrial monitoring.
